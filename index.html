Quick answers

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Who is personal training best for?

Beginners who want confidence with technique, experienced lifters who feel stuck, and anyone who wants structured coaching, accountability, and safe progress.

Do you train beginners?

Yes. You’ll learn safe setup, proper form, and how to progress without feeling overwhelmed by equipment or routines.

Where do sessions take place?

In El Segundo or at your location in the South Bay area. Remote coaching is also available for anywhere.

How do you personalize the program?

We start with an assessment, then build a plan around goals, mobility, schedule, and recovery—updated as you progress.

Do you help with fat loss?

Yes—through training structure, consistency, and sustainable habits. If needed, I can recommend nutrition professionals.

How do I get started?

Book an assessment, tell me your goals, and we’ll choose the best plan—1-on-1, mobility class, or remote coaching.
