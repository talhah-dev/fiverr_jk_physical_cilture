What you’ll work on

The goal is simple: move better so you can lift better. Expect a structured session with drills that improve positions, control, and comfort under load.

  • Improve mobility and flexibility for better movement quality

  • Get into stronger, safer positions for squats, deadlifts, presses

  • Improve lifting form and mechanics

  • Decrease pain and tightness with consistent practice
