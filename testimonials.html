Why supervised training works

Research consistently shows that supervised strength training leads to greater strength gains than unsupervised programs.

The Influence of Direct Supervision of Resistance Training on Strength Performance

Journal: Medicine & Science in Sports & Exercise (2000)

This study found that individuals training under direct supervision experienced significantly greater increases in maximal strength, training load progression, and overall performance compared to unsupervised training.

Mazzetti SA et al. PMID: 10862549
